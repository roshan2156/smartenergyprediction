ML Flow Pipeline

Our intelligent system follows a systematic machine learning pipeline to deliver accurate energy predictions.

1

Data Collection

Collect energy dataset from CSV files and IoT sensors

2

Data Cleaning

Handle missing values, remove errors and outliers

3

Data Preprocessing

Convert categorical to numeric, normalize data

4

Feature Selection

Select important columns (power, time, occupancy)

5

Train-Test Split

Split data into training (80%) and testing (20%) sets

6

Reshape for LSTM

Convert data into sequence format for time-series analysis

7

Model Training

Train Random Forest & LSTM models on historical data

8

Model Evaluation

Check accuracy using R² score and MAE metrics

9

Prediction

Predict energy consumption, cost, and carbon emission

10

Save Model

Save trained model for future predictions
